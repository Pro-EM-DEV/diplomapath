👋 Welcome to DiplomaPath

About Our Platform

Empowering your technical career journey

DiplomaPath India was created with a singular purpose: to bring transparency, clarity, and expert guidance to students pursuing technical education after 10th and 12th grade. We understand that choosing the right polytechnic college and engineering branch is one of the most critical decisions in a student's life.

Our platform provides verified, up-to-date information on over 50+ top colleges, including detailed fee structures, placement records, and admission procedures. Powered by our AI Career Guru and seasoned industry experts, we aim to guide every single student toward a bright, secure, and successful engineering career.

🔭

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted and accessible platform for technical education guidance — empowering every student from every corner of the country to discover their ideal diploma college and build a successful career in engineering and technology. We envision a future where no deserving student misses out on quality technical education due to lack of information or guidance.

🎯

Our Mission

To provide accurate, comprehensive, and unbiased information about India's best diploma and polytechnic colleges — including courses, fees, placements, and admission processes. We are committed to bridging the gap between students and quality institutions through personalized career counseling, industry expertise, and a deep understanding of India's evolving technical education landscape.

📝 Step-by-Step

How to Get Admission

Follow these steps to secure your seat

📋

Research & Shortlist

Compare colleges based on courses, fees, placements, location and accreditation.

📄

Check Eligibility

Most diplomas need 10th pass with 35%+ in Maths & Science. 12th/ITI for lateral entry.

✍️

Register for Entrance

Register for state CET — AP POLYCET, Delhi CET, JEECUP, DTE Maharashtra CAP etc.

📝

Appear for Exam

Prepare with previous year papers. Most test Maths, Science & English.

🎯

Attend Counseling

Based on rank, choose colleges and branches during counseling. Fill choices wisely.

📑

Document Verification

Submit originals: 10th marksheet, TC, caste certificate, Aadhaar, photos.

💳

Pay Fees & Confirm

Pay admission fee. Check for TFWS, SC/ST fee waivers, minority quotas.

🎉

Start Your Journey!

Attend orientation, get ID card, begin your diploma journey!
